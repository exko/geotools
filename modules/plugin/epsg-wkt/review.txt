org.geotools.referencing.crs
----------------------------
Code contains a basic property file based EPSG Authority, in addition to an an AUTO Authority handling the AUTO projects
defined by the OGC WMS specification. The WKT definitions contained are a derrived data product from the
PostGIS project, modifed to hack around limitations in early GeoTools.

- epsg.properties data a derived data product from PostGIS and/or EPSG, license unclear
